Workoo is a freelance platform that I developed to connect freelancers with clients through an intelligent and user-friendly interface. The platform allows users to create profiles, publish and manage projects, and collaborate efficiently. Workoo is designed to simplify the freelancing process and empower both professionals and businesses. .

Workoo offers a streamlined and intelligent job board experience for freelancers and clients. Freelancers benefit from a tailored project-matching system, smart notifications, and an AI-powered assistant that helps them navigate opportunities more efficiently. Clients enjoy advanced filtering, real-time analytics, and easy project tracking. The platform promotes transparency, fast communication, and productivity, making it easier for both sides to connect, collaborate, and succeed.

Clients post project offers on Workoo, and freelancers submit their proposals in response. The clients then review these proposals and have the option to accept or reject them. Once a proposal is accepted, the freelancer can start working on the project.
